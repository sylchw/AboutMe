About Me




Hi!
Here's more about me.

I’m currently matriculated as a Year 3 Computer Engineering Student in NUS. As an adventurous person who loves to take on challenges, I am drawn towards things that I have yet to experience. Throughout the past few years, I have dabbled in music, travelling and some sports, and I’m glad that this has allowed me to find and focus on what I love to do most.

I first plunged into the start-up scene of Singapore in 2016 by joining a 3-month intensive boot camp which brought my team and me from ideation to MVP to pitching. Even though we did not receive the grant in the end, coming in as the top 10 finalists allowed me to make valuable friendships with people which I learnt so much from. Since then, I am drawn towards the purpose of finding solutions for the problems found throughout the world and have been working hard on it. This fuels my passion for entrepreneurship, and to be part of the next biggest idea and solution.

Besides entrepreneurship, I fell in love with travelling in 2017. With some money saved up after my 2 years of National Service, I embarked on a 2-month long backpacking trip with 2 of my friends from Singapore to Russia, travelling mostly by land. The different cultures and lifestyles that I experienced left a deep unsatisfied thirst in my heart, as I sought to learn from the locals that I have met.

Right now, as a person, I aim to live life to the fullest, always trying my best and to experience as many things as I can, while being caring, honest and empathetic.
